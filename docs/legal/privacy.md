# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## 1. Introduction

SpotOnSight operates a location-based social platform. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (DSG) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

## 2. Data We Collect

### Personal Data
- **Account information**: username, email, display name, profile picture, bio
- **Location data**: your geographic coordinates (only with explicit consent)
- **Content**: spots, comments, meetups, and messages you create
- **Usage data**: interactions, favorites, and activity logs

### Automatically Collected
- IP address (stored for security and moderation)
- Device information (browser, OS)
- Access timestamps

## 3. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your data for the following purposes:
- Providing location-based social networking services
- Account management and authentication
- Content moderation and safety
- Improving our services
- Responding to support requests

## 4. Legal Basis

- **Consent**: Location data, marketing communications
- **Contract**: Service delivery, account management
- **Legitimate interest**: Security, moderation, service improvement
- **Legal obligation**: Tax/accounting requirements

## 5. Data Storage

- **Storage location**: Switzerland (primary), EU (backup)
- **Retention period**: 
  - Active accounts: Duration of membership
  - Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
  - Logs: 90 days for security purposes

## 6. Third-Party Services

- **MongoDB**: Database hosting (Switzerland/EU)
- **OpenStreetMap**: Map tiles (EU-based servers)
- **Caddy**: HTTPS termination

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

## 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR/DSG, you have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Export your data in machine-readable format
- **Withdrawal**: Revoke consent at any time

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at the address below.
